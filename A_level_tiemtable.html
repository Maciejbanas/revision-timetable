🧬 Biology Strategy (C → A)

  • Topics 1–4 during school weeks (Mar–Easter), Topics 5–8 over Easter
  • After each topic: Anki flashcards for every definition + process
  • Topic-by-topic Qs on Save My Exams — not full papers yet
  • Paper 1: Biological molecules, Cells, Exchange, Genetic info
  • Paper 2: Energy transfers, Responding, Genetics, Ecosystems
  • Paper 3: Any topic + all 12 required practicals — drill weekly
  • Full past papers from mid-April onwards

📐 Maths Strategy (B → A*)

  • Topic questions ONLY — no past papers until all topics covered
  • Save My Exams topic banks — do every question in each topic
  • Weakest topics first, ask teacher which to prioritise
  • Pure 1 + 2 first, then Stats, then Mechanics
  • Once teacher approves → timed past papers to build exam technique
  • A* differentiation: proof, hardest integration, parametric

💻 CS Strategy (B → A)

  • Paper 1: CPU arch, OS, networking, databases, security, ethics, legal
  • Paper 2: Data structures, sorting/searching, OOP, languages, theory
  • Write pseudocode + algorithms by hand in every session — essential
  • Trace through code execution on paper (exam requires this)
  • Past paper Qs from mid-April for Paper 1 + 2 individually
  • Note exact mark-scheme wording — CS marking is very specific
